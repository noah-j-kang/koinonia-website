Creed

What we believe defines how we live together.

  • • We believe in God the Father, Almighty, Creator of the heavens and the earth.
  • • We believe in the Lord Jesus Christ, the Son of God, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary, and that He died on the cross for our sins and was raised from the dead.
  • • We believe in the Holy Spirit, the presence and the power of God in the life of every believer.
  • • We believe the Bible is the Word of God, our supreme and final authority in all matters of faith and life.
  • • We believe in the fellowship of all believers who follow Jesus as Lord and Savior.
